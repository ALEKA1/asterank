How it works

Asterank has compiled hundreds of thousands of sky survey images for public review.

To discover an asteroid:

  1. Move images on top of one another, lining up stars. (This is done automatically now)
  2. You'll be shown an animation of the night sky. Do you see any moving objects?
  3. The first user to mark an interesting image gets discovery credit and potential naming rights.
